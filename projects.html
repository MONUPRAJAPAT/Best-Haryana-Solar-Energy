How It Works

From first call to switch-on — five steps.

No confusion, no surprise costs. Here's exactly how a project moves from your enquiry to the day your meter starts running backwards.

1

Free Site Survey

Our engineer comes to you, measures the roof, checks shadows and confirms your load and DISCOM tariff.

2

Custom Quote

A clear proposal with system sizing, panel and inverter brand options, transparent pricing and timeline.

3

Subsidy Paperwork

We file your PM Surya Ghar application, upload documents, and follow up with the portal — no running around for you.

4

Installation

Our certified team installs in 1 to 2 days using hot-dip galvanized structure, quality cabling and proper earthing.

5

Commissioning & Support

DISCOM inspection, bidirectional meter, and 5 years of maintenance support included with every system.
